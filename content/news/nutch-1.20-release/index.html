Nutch 1.20 Release

This news announces the Apache Nutch 1.20 release.

    The Apache Nutch PMC are pleased to announce the immediate release of Apache Nutch v1.20, we advise all current users and developers of the 1.X series to upgrade to this release.

    Download Nutch 1.20

    You may also be interested in the 1.20 release report.

    In the 1.X series, release artifacts are made available as both source and binary and also available within Maven Central.
